5S for a Productive Workplace

1S: Sort (Seiri)

Eliminate unnecessary items from the workplace.

  • Identify and remove obsolete tools and equipment.
  • Discard outdated materials and paperwork.
  • Dispose of broken machinery and unused spare parts.

2S: Set in Order (Seiton)

Organize essential items for easy access and retrieval.

  • Establish designated storage locations.
  • Utilize visual aids like labels and shadow boards.

3S: Shine (Seiso)

Maintain a clean and hygienic work environment.

  • Develop a cleaning schedule for equipment, tools, and workspace.
  • Encourage employee participation in cleaning activities.

4S: Standardize (Seiketsu)

Maintain the gains achieved through the first three S's.

  • Document work instructions for sorting, organizing, and cleaning.
  • Implement visual controls like color coding and signage.

5S: Sustain (Shitsuke)

Foster a culture of continuous improvement.

  • Regularly audit and assess adherence to 5S principles.
  • Encourage employee suggestions for improvement.
